Basketball

Basketball Overview

Basketball is a fast-paced game played by two teams of five players each. It is played on a rectangular court with a hoop at each end. The objective is to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop.

Known for its athleticism, skill, and strategy, basketball is immensely popular, especially in the United States and has a growing global following.

Key Highlights:

  • NBA (National Basketball Association) is the premier professional league
  • Major international tournaments include the Olympics and FIBA World Cup
  • Players known for their skill and athleticism like Michael Jordan, LeBron James, and Kobe Bryant
