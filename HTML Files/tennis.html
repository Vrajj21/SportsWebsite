Tennis

Tennis Overview

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles). Each player uses a racket to hit a felt-covered rubber ball over a net into the opponent's court.

It is a popular sport worldwide, known for its competitive nature, skill, and endurance.

Key Highlights:

  • Four Grand Slam tournaments: Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon, and US Open
  • Players like Roger Federer, Rafael Nadal, Serena Williams, and Naomi Osaka have made significant impacts on the sport
  • Tennis is played on various surfaces including grass, clay, and hard courts
