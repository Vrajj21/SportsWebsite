Soccer Overview

Soccer, also known as football in many parts of the world, is the most popular sport globally. It is played between two teams of eleven players each on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal.

Soccer is known for its simplicity and universal appeal, with millions of fans and players worldwide.

Key Highlights:

  • Played in over 200 countries
  • World Cup is the most prestigious tournament
  • Major leagues include English Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, and MLS
